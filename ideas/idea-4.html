Idea 4

AI Research & Insights Labs for SMBs

A lightweight research lab for small and medium businesses: on-demand market intelligence, customer insights, and trend analysis powered by AI instead of a full in-house team.

Most SMBs never get around to proper research. They rely on gut feeling, scattered spreadsheets, and outdated reports. This idea turns AI into their 24/7 insights team.

Problem

Big companies pay consultants and research firms to monitor markets, customers, and competitors. SMBs usually can’t:

  • No budget for dedicated research staff or agencies.
  • Data is fragmented across tools: CRM, website, socials, reviews, support inbox.
  • Decisions are made on intuition instead of live data and structured analysis.

Solution

An “AI Research & Insights Lab” subscription that plugs into the SMB’s existing tools and delivers clear answers instead of raw dashboards:

  • Continuous monitoring of reviews, social media, and competitors’ pages.
  • Monthly or weekly “Insight Packs” in plain language: what changed, what to worry about, what to try.
  • Customer segmentation and churn-risk signals surfaced automatically.
  • One-click deep dives: “Why are we losing leads from channel X?”

The AI does the heavy lifting; humans (you) curate, sanity-check and highlight the real actions.

Why Us

With backgrounds across ETH Zurich, RWTH Aachen, Brown, IIT and real-world SaaS (e.g. ElectriStay), we’re used to turning messy data into decisions. This lab formalizes that capability for SMBs that can’t build a full analytics team.

Snapshot

Status: Idea → Early validation.

Target users:

  • Service SMBs (hospitality, EV-friendly hotels, agencies).
  • Small e-commerce brands.
  • Founders who hate dashboards but love clear recommendations.

Business Model

  • Monthly retainer per company (tiers by data volume & reporting frequency).
  • Setup fee for integrations and initial data audit.
  • Optional “project sprints” for deep dives (e.g. new market, new product).

Tech Stack (Draft)

  • Data ingestion from CRM / web analytics / socials / review platforms.
  • Vector database + retrieval for text data (feedback, reviews, notes).
  • LLMs for summarisation, clustering, and insight drafting.
  • Simple web app for reports + Slack / email digests.
