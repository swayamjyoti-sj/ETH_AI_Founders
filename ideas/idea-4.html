Idea 4

AI Research & Insights Labs for SMBs

A “fractional research lab” for small and medium businesses: on-demand market intelligence, customer insights, and competitor analysis powered by AI, curated by humans.

Instead of guessing or buying expensive consulting reports, SMBs get a lightweight lab that constantly watches their market and turns messy data into clear recommendations.

Problem

Serious market research is a luxury. Large companies hire analysts and agencies; SMBs run on intuition, scattered spreadsheets, and outdated reports:

  • No budget for a dedicated research / strategy team.
  • Customer feedback is fragmented across email, CRM, reviews, and support tickets.
  • Competitive and market changes are spotted late, if at all.

Decisions about pricing, positioning, and product roadmap are often made in the dark.

Solution

An “AI Research & Insights Lab” subscription that plugs into the tools SMBs already use and delivers plain-language insight packs instead of raw dashboards:

  • Ingests data from CRM, website analytics, reviews, social media, and support tickets.
  • Clusters feedback into themes: feature requests, churn reasons, objections, compliments.
  • Monitors competitors’ websites, pricing pages, and public releases.
  • Delivers weekly or monthly briefings: “What changed, why it matters, what to do next.”
  • Allows ad-hoc questions: “Are trial users from channel X lower quality than Y?”

AI handles collection and first-pass analysis; humans review and highlight the actions.

Example Use Cases

  • A boutique SaaS sees which features drive upgrades versus churn and adjusts roadmap.
  • An EV-friendly hotel chain understands why guests choose them over competitors.
  • An e-commerce brand catches a new competitor undercutting them in a key region.

Why Us

Our backgrounds across ETH Zurich, RWTH Aachen, Brown, IIT and SaaS (e.g. ElectriStay) sit at the intersection of deep technical work and real-world operations. We’re used to taking noisy data—experiments, user feedback, system logs—and turning it into decisions.

This idea packages that capability into a repeatable lab offering for SMBs who can’t afford a full strategy or data team.

Snapshot

Status: Idea → Early validation.

Target users:

  • Service SMBs (hospitality, EV-friendly hotels, agencies).
  • Small SaaS and e-commerce teams.
  • Founders who dislike dashboards but want clear, data-backed recommendations.

Business Model

  • Monthly subscription per company, tiered by data volume and report frequency (e.g. Lite, Pro, Plus).
  • One-time deep-dive projects (e.g. pricing study, new market entry analysis).
  • Optional “board pack” add-on – investor-ready quarterly insight reports.

Tech Stack (Draft)

  • Data ingestion via APIs from CRM, ticketing, analytics, review platforms.
  • Warehouse (Postgres / Snowflake / BigQuery) + dbt or similar for modeling.
  • Vector database for unstructured text (feedback, reviews, call transcripts).
  • LLMs for summarization, clustering, and narrative report generation.
  • Web app for dashboards + PDF / email / Slack report delivery.
