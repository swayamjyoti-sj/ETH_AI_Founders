Idea 7

AI Lab-to-Market Scout

An AI scout that scans scientific papers, theses, patents, and internal reports to surface commercializable ideas, potential products, and startup concepts for universities and R&D-heavy companies.

Instead of great research dying in PDFs and internal drives, teams get a prioritized list of “this could be a product” opportunities with suggested markets and next steps.

Problem

Universities and R&D labs are full of IP and prototypes that never leave the lab:

  • Tech transfer offices are understaffed and can’t deeply read every paper or thesis.
  • Researchers are rewarded for publications, not for commercialization.
  • Industry partners don’t know what’s being developed until it’s too late or too early.

As a result, billions in potential value remains “hidden” in PDFs, lab notebooks, and internal slides.

Solution

An AI “Lab-to-Market Scout” that continuously ingests research outputs and flags high-potential commercialization candidates:

  • Reads papers, theses, patents, proposals, and project reports.
  • Classifies work by technology area, maturity, and potential applications.
  • Maps each item to possible markets, use-cases, and existing companies.
  • Scores opportunities on impact, readiness, and differentiation.
  • Produces concise opportunity briefs for tech transfer, founders, and investors.

Human experts review the top-ranked candidates and decide what to pursue.

Example Workflow

  • Connect institutional repositories, preprint servers, and patent databases.
  • AI ingests new material weekly and updates an opportunity pipeline.
  • Tech transfer staff receive a shortlist: “Top 10 commercialization candidates this quarter.”
  • Each candidate has: a one-page brief, potential markets, competitor overview, and risks.
  • Selected projects move into deeper diligence or spin-out support.

Why Us

With roots at ETH Zurich, RWTH Aachen, Brown, and IIT, we live in environments where high-value research often stops at publication. We also have direct experience with industry collaboration and scientific productization.

This product is a natural bridge between our academic background and our startup ambitions: systematically surfacing “hidden” opportunities and helping push them toward the market.

Snapshot

Status: Idea / long-term bet.

Target users:

  • University tech transfer & innovation offices.
  • Corporate R&D teams and venture studios.
  • Deep-tech VCs scouting university pipelines.

Business Model

  • B2B SaaS for institutions (annual licenses).
  • Premium tiers for custom models, private deployments, and integrations.
  • Success fees or equity slices on spin-outs (where appropriate).

Tech Stack (Draft)

  • Pipelines for ingesting PDFs, LaTeX, and patent formats.
  • Text embeddings + vector DB for semantic search over research corpora.
  • LLMs for summarization, classification, and opportunity-brief generation.
  • Web dashboard with filters by domain, TRL, and market.
