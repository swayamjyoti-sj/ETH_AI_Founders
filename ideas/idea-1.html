Idea 1

AI-Powered Knowledge Product Studios

A studio that turns complex topics into clear, structured knowledge products: explainers, mini-courses, and step-by-step guides, powered by AI and expert review.

Example output: YouTube explainers, cohort-style mini-courses, and actionable guides that compress months of learning into hours.

Problem

People don’t pay for raw information anymore – they pay for clarity, structure, and context. Subject-matter experts have the knowledge but lack the time and tooling to productize it.

  • Researchers and engineers struggle to turn expertise into courses or digital products.
  • Audiences drown in unstructured content (papers, talks, random videos).
  • Traditional course production is slow, expensive, and often low-quality.

Solution

An AI-assisted studio that helps experts go from idea → structured outline → scripts → slides → launch. Humans define the insight; AI handles the heavy lifting.

  • AI-generated outlines and draft scripts from expert prompts and notes.
  • Automatic slide / visual suggestions based on the structure.
  • Templates for YouTube, cohort courses, and written playbooks.
  • Built-in QA: checklists to ensure rigor, citations, and accuracy.

Why Us

As researchers and teachers across ETH Zurich, RWTH Aachen, Brown, IIT, and ETH Zürich’s broader ecosystem, we already design lectures, workshops, and research talks. This product turns that workflow into software for others.

Snapshot

Status: Idea → Spec

Target users: Researchers, engineers, consultants, and niche educators.

Business model:

  • SaaS subscription for solo creators and small teams.
  • Studio service: “Done-with-you” course / content build.

Tech Stack (Draft)

  • Frontend: React / Next.js.
  • Backend: Python (FastAPI) or Node.js.
  • LLMs: OpenAI / Anthropic APIs for drafting and structuring.
  • Storage: Postgres + object storage for assets.
