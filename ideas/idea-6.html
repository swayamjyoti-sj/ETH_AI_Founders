Idea 6

AI Onboarding & Training Coach for Teams

An AI coach that turns your existing documentation, tools, and workflows into interactive onboarding and training paths for new team members.

Instead of sending people a giant Notion space or Confluence wiki, you give them a guided, conversational coach that answers questions and tracks real progress.

Problem

Onboarding and training are usually chaotic:

  • New hires get a flood of links, docs, and Slack channels.
  • Knowledge lives in the heads of a few senior people who don’t have time.
  • Managers can’t see who is actually ramping up and who is lost.
  • Teams repeat the same explanations again and again.

Result: slow ramp-up, uneven knowledge, and frustration on both sides.

Solution

An AI Onboarding & Training Coach that layers on top of your existing stack and turns static information into a guided experience:

  • Ingests company docs, code repos, internal FAQs, and process docs.
  • Builds role-specific learning paths (e.g. “Backend engineer”, “Customer success”).
  • Lets new team members learn via chat-style Q&A instead of hunting in wikis.
  • Includes quizzes, mini-projects, and check-ins to confirm understanding.
  • Gives managers a simple view of progress and knowledge gaps.

Example Workflow

  • Connect Notion / Confluence, GitHub, Google Drive, and internal FAQs.
  • Define roles and seniority levels (e.g. Junior BE, Senior FE, Data Analyst).
  • AI proposes an onboarding path for each role, which you can edit and approve.
  • New hires log in and follow the path: reading, watching, doing small tasks, asking questions.
  • The coach tracks what they’ve covered and where they struggle, and suggests follow-ups.

Why Us

We’ve designed and delivered courses, workshops, and research supervision across ETH Zurich, RWTH Aachen, Brown, IIT, and beyond. We know how people actually learn technical material.

This product is a natural extension: combining our teaching background with AI to help companies train people faster, without burning out their seniors.

Snapshot

Status: Idea → MVP concept.

Target users:

  • Tech startups hiring engineers and data scientists.
  • Customer support / success teams with complex products.
  • Universities and training programmes onboarding tutors / TAs.

Business Model

  • SaaS pricing per active learner, with a minimum seat count.
  • Higher tiers for SSO, HRIS integrations, and custom analytics dashboards.
  • One-off onboarding “design sprints” as a services add-on.

Tech Stack (Draft)

  • Connectors to Notion / Confluence / Google Drive / GitHub.
  • Backend: Python / Node.js with Postgres + vector database.
  • LLMs for retrieval-augmented Q&A and learning path generation.
  • Frontend: React / Next.js with a chat-style interface and admin dashboard.
