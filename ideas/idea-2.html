Idea 2

Industry-Specific AI Automation Agencies

Focused AI agencies that go deep into one vertical – like hospitality, EV charging, or materials – and automate high-value workflows end-to-end.

Instead of generic “AI consulting”, each agency solves a narrow but painful problem for one industry, combining software + services.

Problem

Businesses want automation, but generic tools rarely fit their specific data, constraints, and regulations. They need AI that understands their industry, not just generic prompts.

  • Operators waste time on repetitive, high-context manual tasks.
  • Internal data is underused (or not used at all) for decision-making.
  • Most AI offerings are either too generic or too custom/expensive.

Solution

Build “vertical AI agencies” that specialize in one niche and ship reusable automations:

  • For hospitality & EV travel: demand forecasting, pricing, and charging-slot optimization.
  • For engineering / R&D teams: automated reporting, experimental data analysis, and documentation.
  • For education: AI-assisted grading, feedback, and learning analytics.

Each vertical gets a playbook, templates, and a growing library of automations maintained by the agency.

Why Us

Between us, we cover physics, materials, mechanics, software, and a live SaaS project in sustainable hospitality (ElectriStay). That gives us unique insight into at least two strong verticals to start with.

Snapshot

Status: Idea → Customer discovery.

First verticals:

  • EV-friendly hospitality (charging + booking).
  • Technical education & training (universities, bootcamps).

Business Model

  • Setup fee for initial automation + integration.
  • Monthly retainer for monitoring, updates, and new automations.
  • Optionally: revenue share tied to efficiency gains.

Tech Stack (Draft)

  • Python orchestration with frameworks like LangChain / LlamaIndex.
  • APIs to CRM / booking / ERP systems.
  • LLMs tuned for each vertical’s language and documents.
