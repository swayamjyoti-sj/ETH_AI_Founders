Idea 3

AI Validation Co-Founder

An AI “co-founder” that helps you pressure-test startup ideas, interview customers, and validate positioning before you spend months building the wrong thing.

Instead of guessing or getting lost in fake “market research”, founders get a focused workflow that turns vague ideas into validated offers fast.

Problem

Most first-time founders don’t fail because they can’t build. They fail because they build before they validate:

  • They talk to the wrong people (friends, colleagues, Twitter), not real buyers.
  • They ask biased questions and get “polite lies” instead of hard truths.
  • They never turn chaotic notes into a clear “yes/no” on the idea.

Result: months of work, landing pages, and features… for a product nobody urgently wants.

Solution

An AI Validation Co-Founder that walks you through a repeatable, evidence-based validation flow:

  • Clarify your hypothesis: market, problem, and who you think will pay.
  • Generate unbiased interview scripts and survey questions automatically.
  • Summarise interview transcripts and survey results into clear insights.
  • Score ideas on demand, urgency, and willingness to pay.
  • Suggest next moves: pivot, refine, niche down, or double down.

The tool doesn’t just “chat” – it enforces a validation process.

Example Workflow

  • Input your idea in 2–3 sentences (or paste a Notion doc).
  • AI identifies assumptions and turns them into testable questions.
  • It generates interview scripts + Typeform/Google Form surveys.
  • You run 5–10 calls / send surveys; paste responses back in.
  • AI clusters responses, extracts patterns, and flags red/green lights.
  • You get a one-page validation report you can show a co-founder or investor.

Snapshot

Status: Idea → MVP.

Target users:

  • Non-technical founders validating SaaS ideas.
  • Solo builders shipping weekend projects.
  • Accelerators & university entrepreneurship programs.

Business Model

  • SaaS: monthly subscription with limited validation “runs”.
  • One-off report: pay-per-project for accelerators & schools.
  • Upsell: hands-on sprints where you guide a batch of founders.

Tech Stack (Draft)

  • Frontend: React / Next.js web app.
  • Backend: Python (FastAPI) or Node.js with a Postgres DB.
  • LLMs: API (OpenAI / Anthropic / etc.) for:
    • Question design (unbiased interviews & surveys).
    • Summarising qualitative responses.
    • Clustering problems, segments, and objections.
  • Optional: integrations with Typeform, Google Forms, Notion.
